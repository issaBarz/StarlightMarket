Welcome to the Starlight Market!

For Artists:

We provide a platform where creators can showcase their work, reach a wider audience, and earn from their designs.

For Customers:

We make it easier to discover, browse, and order stickers you'll love, all without the fear of buying something that was generated by AI.

For the Community:

We celebrate real artists and the beauty of their skills. Despite what some people wish to believe, AI can NEVER replace true art!

Our Goal

Stickers are more than just decorations, they are special ways to express yourself. Many online marketplaces have been flooded with AI generated works.

You deserve stickers made with love, not soulless, machine-generated slop.

Here at Starlight Market, our goal is to connect artists and sticker collectors through a space where every design has a chance to shine despite the growing development of generative AI.
